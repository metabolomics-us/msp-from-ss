MSP From Spreadsheet

Convert a spreadsheet of MS/MS data into a .msp file.

Click 'Browse' and select an excel or csv file. Then click 'Submit' to download a converted .msp text file.

Your spreadsheet should include columns with these labels: 'Average Rt(min)', 'Average Mz', 'Metabolite name', 'Adduct type', 'Formula', 'INCHIKEY', 'MS1 isotopic spectrum', and 'MS/MS spectrum'. It may include other columns, but those will not be included in the final product. Spelling is important but capitalization is not.
